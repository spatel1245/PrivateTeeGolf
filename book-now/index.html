Before You Book

A few things worth knowing.

Do I need to bring my own clubs?

Yes — bring your own clubs. The simulator, projector, and software are all on and ready when you arrive. Just show up with your bag.

Is anyone there when I arrive?

The facility is unstaffed — that's intentional. You get the entire space to yourself with zero interruptions. If you ever need anything, reach us at (878) 645-3216.

What if I've never used a simulator before?

No experience needed. Everything is on when you walk in — just pick a course on screen and start playing. If you'd like a walkthrough before your visit, contact us and we'll help.

How many people can play?

Up to 4 players comfortably. The $35/hr rate covers your whole group — not per person.

Do I need a membership?

No. Book and pay per session — no membership, no commitment. If you end up playing regularly, memberships offer included hours at a better rate.
