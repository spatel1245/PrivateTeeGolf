Presale Details

Founding Membership & Waitlist Information

1. What the Presale Includes

Our presale is designed for golfers who want early access to Private Tee Golf. By joining the waitlist, you are reserving priority access to Founding Membership pricing and availability when we open in Greensburg, PA.

We will offer both annual and monthly memberships. Monthly memberships will automatically renew until cancelled in accordance with our Terms & Conditions.

  • Priority access to membership enrollment before the general public.
  • Access to limited Founding Membership rates for Starter, Practice, and Ultimate tiers.
  • Updates on construction progress, opening date, and booking timeline.
  • Clear communication of annual and monthly membership options before billing begins.

2. Billing Start Date & Preview Sessions

Presale memberships are scheduled to begin billing on March 20, 2026. You will not be billed prior to this date for your membership dues.

Ahead of your first billing date, you may be invited for a complimentary 30-minute preview session so you can experience the facility, technology, and layout before your membership officially starts.

If you change your mind before billing begins, simply let us know and we will cancel your presale membership and remove you from the waitlist.

3. Membership Tiers & Included Hours

All membership tiers include a set number of simulator hours each month. These hours reset every billing cycle and unused hours do not roll over month-to-month.

  • Starter — Includes 4 hours of simulator time per month.
  • Practice — Includes 10 hours of simulator time per month.
  • Ultimate — Includes at least 20 hours of simulator time per month, with additional access based on availability as described on the main page.

If you consistently need more time than your tier provides, you can purchase additional hours (when available) or discuss upgrading your membership tier.

For a visual overview of pricing and benefits, see the Membership Tiers section on the homepage.

4. How We Use Your Information

When you join the presale waitlist, we collect basic contact information (such as name, email, and phone number) so we can send you updates about membership, construction progress, and booking availability.

For full details about how we collect, use, and protect your data, please review our Privacy Policy.

5. SMS & Email Updates

By providing your phone number and email on the waitlist form, you agree that we may send you presale-related updates, including membership information, opening announcements, and instructions for activating your account. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out of marketing messages at any time, but may still receive essential or legally required notifications.

The specific SMS consent language and terms are outlined in our Terms & Conditions.

6. Next Steps

If you have not yet joined the presale waitlist, you can do so from the main page using the form in the Membership Waitlist section.

  • Review the membership tiers on the Membership Tiers section.
  • Join the waitlist with your preferred tier selection.
  • Watch for email and SMS updates as we approach opening.
