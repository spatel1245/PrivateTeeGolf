How to Use the Simulator

A quick guide for when you arrive at your booked session.

1 You’ll Receive a Code

Before your session, we’ll text you a 6-digit door code. Keep it handy—you’ll need it when you arrive.

2 Enter the Code & Open the Door

When you arrive at your scheduled time, enter your 6-digit code on the keypad, then press # to unlock the door.

🚧 We’re still undergoing exterior renovations — the door area may look unfinished in this photo.

The parking area right in front of the building is open for guests.

Tip: Enter the code exactly as texted, then press #.

3 Start Playing

The computer, TV, and projector will already be on and running GSPRO. Select Local Match to jump into a course or head to the driving range for practice.

4 Course Not Downloaded?

If the course you want isn’t available, press Download to add it and wait for it to load before playing. Or text us and we’ll have it ready for your next visit.

5 Ready to Hit

Before you swing, make sure the Uneekor logo on the launch monitor mounted to the ceiling lights up green, and the screen shows Connected in green.

6 Wrapping Up

When your time is up, the simulator will automatically power down. Please clean up the space and exit by the end of your booked time.
