First time?

Common questions

Is anyone there when I arrive?

No, the facility is unstaffed on purpose. Everything turns on by itself, the lights, simulator, and TV included, so the bay is ready the moment you walk in. It's yours the whole time you're booked, and if you need anything we're a text or call away at (878) 645-3216.

How do I get into the facility?

We text you a code 15 minutes before your session. Punch it into the keypad and you're in. No key, no app.

What if I've never used a golf simulator before?

Everything's on and ready when you walk in. Pick a course on the screen and swing. Want a walkthrough first? Send us a message.

Do I need to bring my own clubs?

Yes, bring your own clubs. Everything else is here and ready.

Can I bring friends?

Bring whoever you want. The $40/hr covers your whole group, so split it however you like.
