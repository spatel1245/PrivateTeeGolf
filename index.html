Common Questions

Everything you need to know before your first visit.

Do I need to bring my own clubs?

Yes — bring your own clubs. Everything else is provided: the simulator is on, the course is loaded, and the bay is ready when you walk in. Just show up with your bag.

Do I need a membership to book?

No. Anyone can book a session at $35/hr — no membership required. Memberships are for regular players who want included hours at a better effective rate.

How do I get into the facility?

After booking, you'll receive a unique door code via SMS 15 minutes before your session. Enter the code at the keypad on the door and you're in. No key, no app, no staff needed.

Is anyone there when I arrive?

The facility is unstaffed — that's intentional. You get the entire space to yourself with no interruptions. If you ever need help during your session, reach us at (878) 645-3216 or info@privateteegolf.com.

What if I've never used a golf simulator before?

No experience needed. When you arrive, the projector, simulator software, and everything else is already on and ready to go — just bring your clubs, pick a course, and swing. If you want a walkthrough before your first session, just reach out and we'll help.

Can I bring friends?

Yes. The bay comfortably fits up to 4 players. Members can bring up to 3 guests at no additional charge. Public bookings cover everyone in your group under the hourly rate.

What hours are you open?

24/7. The smart access system means there are no set open hours — book any time that works for you, any day of the week.

How do I cancel or modify a booking?

Contact us directly at info@privateteegolf.com or (878) 645-3216 and we'll take care of it.

Ready to Play?
