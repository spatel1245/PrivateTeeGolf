How To Use Your Membership

A simple step-by-step guide to Private Tee Golf.

1. Join the Waitlist

Start by reserving your spot on the Founding Membership waitlist. This gives you priority access to membership enrollment and founder pricing when we open in Greensburg, PA.

  • Visit the Membership Waitlist section on the homepage.
  • Select your preferred membership tier (Starter, Practice, or Ultimate).
  • Submit your contact information so we can send you presale and opening details.

2. Confirm Your Membership

As we approach opening, you’ll receive an email and/or SMS with steps to finalize your membership.

  • Review the membership options and included hours on the Membership Tiers section.
  • Confirm whether you want a monthly or annual membership. Monthly memberships will auto-renew until cancelled.
  • Review our Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy.

3. Book Simulator Time

Once your membership is active, you can start booking your simulator sessions online.

  • Log in to the booking system using the credentials we provide.
  • Select the date, time, and duration of your session based on your available membership hours.
  • Confirm your booking and watch for a confirmation email or SMS.

Remember: each membership tier includes a fixed number of hours per month, and unused hours do not roll over to the next month.

4. Access the Facility

Private Tee Golf is designed for secure, convenient 24/7 access using smart entry.

  • Before your scheduled session, you’ll receive a unique door code via SMS.
  • Arrive at your scheduled time (not early), enter your code, and the door will unlock for your session.
  • Do not open the door for other guests outside your party; this can disrupt the automation system.

For full access and conduct expectations, see our Terms & Conditions.

5. Enjoy Your Session

Once inside, you’ll have private access to the simulator bay and lounge area for the duration of your booking.

  • Follow the on-screen prompts to select courses, practice modes, or games.
  • Use clean clubs and balls, and remove any balls that collect near the screen.
  • Treat the space, equipment, and walls with care so we can keep the environment premium for every member.

6. Need Help?

If you ever have questions about booking, billing, or using the facility:

  • Email us at info@privateteegolf.com.
  • Use the contact form on the Contact page.
  • Review policies on the Terms & Conditions and Presale Details pages.
