How to Book Using Your Membership Credits

1 Choose your time and bay

After you select a start time on the calendar, you'll see the Reserving Time screen. Here you can adjust your Time Length, keep the Private Bay selected, and then click the Rate row to choose the member rate and apply your membership credits on the next screens.

2 Select the Members rate & sign in

On the Select Items screen, tap the Members rate and then choose Sign In. Use the same phone number you used when purchasing your membership. You'll receive a quick text message with a code to confirm it's you, and then your credits will be available to apply.

3 Apply your membership credits

Next, you'll see the Select Credits screen. Here you can see how many membership credits you have remaining for the month and choose how many to use. Tap Apply next to the credit option to apply it to this booking, then choose Done to return to checkout.

4 Confirm & reserve for $0

Back on the Reserving Time screen, you'll now see your Membership Credits applied and the total set to $0.00. When everything looks correct (time length, bay, and credits), click Reserve $0.00 to complete your free booking using your monthly membership quota. No charge will be made as long as the total remains $0.00.
