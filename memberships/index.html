How It Works

Monthly hours, covered

Your monthly fee covers all your included simulator hours. No per-session charges on top — just book and play.

Book online, anytime

Schedule sessions through the Birrdi booking portal. You'll receive a unique SMS door code 15 minutes before each session.

Need more hours?

Members can add discounted extra hours beyond their monthly allowance. You can also upgrade your tier at any time.

Common Questions

Do unused hours roll over?

Hours reset each billing cycle and don't roll over. If you consistently need more, you can upgrade your tier or purchase discounted extra hours.

Can I upgrade or cancel later?

Yes. You can upgrade at any time. Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period. Contact us to make changes.

How do I book sessions once I'm a member?

After purchase you'll have access to the Birrdi member portal to book time slots using your included hours. You'll receive a step-by-step guide, or see our booking guide for a walkthrough.

What's the difference between annual and monthly?

Annual billing is charged upfront for the year and includes one month free — effectively 12 months for the price of 11. Monthly billing renews each month and can be cancelled at any time.
